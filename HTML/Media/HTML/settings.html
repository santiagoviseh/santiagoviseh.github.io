South Park Elementary

South Park Elementary is the main school in South Park. It is one of the oldest known locations on the show and has appeared in three incarnations.

Cartman's House

Cartman's house is built similarly to the other houses in South Park, painted in different colors.

Kentucky Fried Chicken

Kentucky Fried Chicken (abbreviated as KFC) is a real fast-food chain restaurant famous for its fried chicken. It is also one of the favored fast food places of the boys and it was first seen in the Season Eight episode, "Cartman's Incredible Gift".
