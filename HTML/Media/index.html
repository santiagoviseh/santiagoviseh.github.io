South Park is an American adult animated sitcom, created by Trey Parker and Matt Stone. Follows the misadventures of four irreverent grade-schoolers in the quiet, dysfunctional town of South Park, Colorado. The curious, adventure-seeking group of 10 year old fourth grade boys,Stan, Kyle, Cartman, and Kenny all join in on buffoonish adventures that sometimes evolve nothing.
